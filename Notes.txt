Beers' Basic Blackjack JS


//vars
cash = money to bet, starts at 1,000
bet = how much is bet on the hand, min = 2, max = 500
teachingMode = on off toggle, if on, then show player all of the cards that have been used and those left in the deck

deck = regular deck of cards

playerCardA = first card, face up
playerCardB = second card, face up
playerScore = score total

houseCardA = first card, face up
houseCardB = second card, face down (ie hidden)
houseScore = score total



Program Actions
1. Place bet
2. Check for naturals
3. Play - Player decides if to Hit and when to Stand
4. Dealer's Play - dealer hits according to rules
5. Announce the cards and settle up
